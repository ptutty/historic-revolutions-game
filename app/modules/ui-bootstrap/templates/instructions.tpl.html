Game Play

Revolutions are the product of a combination of factors, in differing proportions. In this game you can play as either a ruler or a revolutionary and decide which factors to play to either cause a revolution to break out or to put the revolution down. There are six possible historical revolutions scenarios that will be given to you based on whether you are a ruler or revolutionary and the difficulty level you choose.

For example, taking a factor such as “The urban masses” you will also be required to give it a rating on a sliding five-point scale depending ranging from:

Contented, in full employment, in a society allowing social mobility

Starving, impoverished, in a society that restricts opportunity

Factors have secret weighing depending on the type of revolution scenario. Some are more important than others in their impact on putting the fire of revolution out or starting a mass uprising.

The object of the game is to:

Revolutionary: Cause a revolution to breakout using the least number of factors.

Ruler: Put down the revolutionary forces using the least number of factors.

The game has 3 levels. On the easiest level you have 12 factors out of 15 to choose from to make a revolution break out or to douse the flames of revolution. The number of factors you can use decreases with each level.
